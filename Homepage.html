Welcome to Little Caesars Arena!

Little Caesars Arena

is an arena located in Midtown Detroit. The arena had its debut on September 5th, 2017. The Red Wings played their inaugural game during the preseason against the Boston Bruins on September 23rd, 2017. Little Caesars Arena is home of the Detroit Red Wings hockey team and the Detroit Pistons basketball team. The arena is a multi-purpose arena which also holds concerts and other sporting events.
